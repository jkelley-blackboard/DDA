lrn_stds_sub_doc

This table contains the standards for a group of the standards within a standards document. Providers normally group standards within a document in one of two ways. For primary grades (approximately 1-8) they group standards by Grade, e.g. Kindergarten or 3rd Grade. For secondary grades (approximately 9-12) they group standards by subject area, e.g. "Algebra 1".
